GAME RULES:
This is a dancing machine game, but it's for your mouse! You can click the whole column when the
dropping rectangles overlap with the buttom rectangles, the more they overlap you will receive more points!
To get extra points, you can try to click the buttom squares instead of some random place of the whole column
Also, you should try to get more combos, when you click too early or too late, your combo will return to zero.
